West Melbourne

East Melbourne is an established, affluent area with many 19th century homes, iconic landmarks and parks. The historic Fitzroy, Treasury and Parliament gardens separate East Melbourne from the central city.

Treasury Gardens

Parliament House, the Old Treasury Building, St Patrick's Catholic Cathedral and the world-famous Melbourne Cricket Ground are all in East Melbourne. Many other Victorian Government facilities and institutions are also located in this area.

While terrace housing is widespread, the area has a growing number of apartments as well as remnant mansions, some of which are listed on the Victorian Heritage Register.

East Melbourne has a large health services industry. Many medical practitioners have rooms in the area because it's close to several hospitals. Local hospitals include The Royal Victorian Eye and Ear Hospital, Freemasons Hospital and Peter MacCallum Cancer Institute.

Residents of East Melbourne are generally older and from an English-speaking background.
