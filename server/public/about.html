WHO WE ARE

Everyone has the right to enjoy the city after dark.

Half of the women in Australia will experience sexual harassment in their lifetime. Women also experience high levels of sexual harassment -- as well as unique forms of aggression relating to their sexuality, such as 'outing' or 'shaming'.

We want all women to feel safe, confident and welcome at all times while they are in Melbourne. This is why we've developed the "Women Safety in Melbourne" Website.

WHAT WE DO

At Women Safety in Melbourne we work to make women feel safer in Melbourne. We do this by providing a list of Suburbs, that we have identified based on crime data, user analytics and feedback, that are high risk zones and should be avoided. We want your website to be highly interactive and user friendly so that our users can get the information very eaily and effieciently, therefore we have provided all our information on a map apart from the Suburbs list.

HOW OUR SYSTEM WORKS

We use a complex algorithm to calculate and rank the safety of suburbs. This ranking is then displayed on the map using a unique colour to depict the risk and other zones. Our map has a unique functionality that allows it to track the current location of the user, this helps you check all desired zones in relation to your current location.

We have a database that stores all the contact information of the users and data for the suburbs and the various zones. This database is regularly updated to keep our data relevent and ensure that there is no compronise on the safety of the users of our application.
